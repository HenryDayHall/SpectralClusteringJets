\begin{abstract}
\noindent
We present a new method of jet definition; an alternative to methods that cluster directly on kinematic data, such as \antikt,
we use kinematic relations to represent the particles in a new multidimensional space for clustering.
This new space is created using the spectral clustering algorithm.
% what is spectral representation? "Represent the particles in a new large multidimentisonal space."
% the algorithm for this is spectral clusteing. so people know what spectral is
 The performance of this algorithm in analysing  
\(gg\to H_{125\,\text{GeV}} \rightarrow H_{40\,\text{GeV}} H_{40\,\text{GeV}} \rightarrow b \bar{b} b \bar{b}\),
\(gg\to H_{500\,\text{GeV}} \rightarrow H_{125\,\text{GeV}} H_{125\,\text{GeV}} \rightarrow b \bar{b} b \bar{b}\)
and
\(gg,q\bar q\to t\bar t\to b\bar b W^+W^-\to b\bar b jj \ell\nu_\ell\) events from 
Monte Carlo (MC) samples in reconstructing the relevant final states is compared to that of the \antikt{} algorithm.
 Measures of infra-red (IR) safety, both soft and collinear, are also studied. We find that the ability of spectral clustering to reconstruct mass peaks is comparable to that of \antikt{}.% under realistic acceptance and selection criteria. << Too non specific
 This process is shown to work on a variety of signal processes without change to it's parameter settings,
 including signals that would need adjusted parameters under \antikt{}.
 % this process is shown to work in a variety of different signal type events.
 % Cut " adapted to the kinematics of the events under study"  this is a much more specific adaption in experimental conditions, so shouldn't be seen as standard practice. 
 \end{abstract}
